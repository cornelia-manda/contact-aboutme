ABOUT

ME

Hi, I'm Cornelia, a photographer and Global Business & Digital Arts (GBDA) student at the University of Waterloo.

My passion for photography and digital creation is rooted in the belief that a photograph and project is a timeless memory. I specialize and love capturing life's most authentic moments, focusing on preserving memories filled with genuine emotion and connection that can be cherished for years to come.

As a GBDA student, I bring a unique blend of artistic vision and digital proficiency to every project. For me, documenting these moments whether for clients or for my personal projects is a continuous, emotional journey.

I thrive on the positive energy of others. When I converse with and capture someone's happiness, that energy and kindness inherently radiates into my own work, creating a joyful and collaborative experience for everyone involved.
